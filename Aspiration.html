My experiences have made it possible for me to get along well regardless of any situation. If I join the company, I will be an employee who maintains the team atmosphere so that everyone can create a culture of respect and consideration for each other. Also, I will try to be an active follower and become an employee who is not afraid of learning. Even if conflicts and incomprehensible difficulties arise, we will try not to avoid them unconditionally, but to become an employee who finds consensus and grows in a relationship of understanding and consideration.
